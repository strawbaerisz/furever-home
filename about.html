WHO WE ARE

We’re a passionate team of animal lovers and volunteers dedicated to rescuing, caring for, and finding loving homes for abandoned pets in Cabuyao City, Laguna.

OUR MISSION

To promote responsible pet adoption and inspire compassion by creating awareness about proper pet care and animal welfare in our community.

WHAT WE DO

We organize adoption drives, provide temporary shelters, and partner with veterinarians to ensure every rescued pet receives proper treatment and love.
