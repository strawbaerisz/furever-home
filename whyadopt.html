WHY ADOPT?

A 2024 report from the Mars Petcare Pet Homelessness Project estimates that there are around 13.11 million stray cats and dogs in the Philippines. These animals roam the streets, often struggling to survive without consistent food, shelter, or the love they deserve. Adopting a stray isn’t just an act of kindness- it’s a life-saving choice.When you open your home to a rescued pet, you don’t just give them safety; you also gain a loyal companion who brings joy, comfort, and unconditional love. Studies even show that pets can help reduce stress and improve emotional well-being. So why wait? Be the reason a stray finds hope again- adopt neow! Pun intended! ;)
